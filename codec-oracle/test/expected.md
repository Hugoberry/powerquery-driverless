## What each result would mean

- A fails, B fails: codec genuinely absent from the engine.
- A works, B fails: codec present but the wrapper is malformed for Microsoft's reader (fix the wrapper; the codec claim still stands).
- A works, B works: Parquet.Document is a decompression oracle for this codec; any format compressing its blocks with it becomes readable in pure M.
- B_uncompressed and B_gzip are wrapper sanity controls: gzip is known-implemented, so if B_gzip fails the wrapper is wrong, not the codec.
